The Destination: Coorg's Captivating Topography

Coorg (Kodagu) is perched on the eastern slopes of the magnificent Western Ghats, offering a dramatic contrast to the plains of Bangalore and Mysore.

  • Mountain Majesty: Home to towering peaks like Tadiandamol, the region is characterized by rolling hills, deep valleys, and verdant slopes that trap the monsoon mist.
  • Coffee Country: The mild, humid climate and high altitude create the perfect environment for sprawling coffee (primarily Robusta) and spice plantations, which carpet the hillsides.
  • River Origins: This area is the birthplace of the revered Kaveri River, with numerous streams and waterfalls (like Abbey Falls) adding to the lush, restorative landscape.
